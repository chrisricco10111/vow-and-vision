About

Vow & Vision is the work of Ricco & Kaceli.

We photograph weddings across Italy and Europe with a single focus: the image. Not the timeline, not the checklist — the moment of it.

Our approach is quiet and present. We move without interruption, read atmosphere before it forms, and work in existing light wherever possible.

The result is a body of work that holds.

Based in Turin · Available internationally

Experience

Working with us is unhurried. We plan carefully, arrive early, and disappear into the day.

24h Preview

A selection of images within 24 hours of the wedding.

7-Day Delivery

Full edited gallery delivered within seven days.

Short Notice

Availability for bookings with less than 30 days notice.

Every booking includes a pre-wedding consultation, print-ready files, and a private online gallery.
